About Us
We’re a company founded in 2018 with the purpose of giving people great Apps And Games

BeeBoyD was founded in 2018 by a guy called Dan. In late 2019 BeeBoyD started making small apps. Due to the pandemic in 2020, BeeBoyD was shut down. BeeBoyD started making apps and games in early 2021. Later we created the "VBS Scripts Pack" which was posted on the site made by BeeBoyD called "Games Mania News". In mid 2022 BeeBoyD created the biggest project that they've worked on. A Minecraft Server called "MCCommandCraft". BeeBoyD will continue to create apps and games.
